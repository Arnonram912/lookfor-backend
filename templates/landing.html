Frequently asked questions

Where is the Admin Office Located?

You can find the admin at 3rd floor of the STI College Novaliches Building.

How long do you keep unclaimed items?

Items are kept for the entire semester. After this period the unclaimed items are disposed according to the school policy.

What proof do I need to claim an item?

You need to present your School ID and be able to describe the unique features of the item.
